📄 design-guidelines.md
Visual Style
Font: Inter

Primary Color: #6A7FDB (calm blue)

Accent: #C2A9FF (soothing lavender)

Background: #FFFFFF and #F7F7F8

Borders & Shadows: Soft shadows, rounded corners (16–24px)

Whitespace: Generous padding and margins; clear section breaks

Layout Principles
Mobile-first

Card-based grouping of information

Sticky headers and breadcrumbs on scroll

“Share” buttons and CTAs clearly styled and spaced

Data tables and pros/cons styled with icons and subtle contrast

Accessibility Notes
High-contrast color pairs for key text

Keyboard navigation friendly

Text sizing scalable (rem/em units)

Alt text for all uploaded images

Motion reduction setting to disable animations

Brand Tone and Voice
Supportive, calming, and empowering

Human and clear — AI speaks like a helpful friend

Experts use a warm, semi-formal tone

Mission-forward: trustworthy advice, not salesy copy
